The Exponential You Assessment

A MadMuscles-grade quiz funnel for mindvalley.com that diagnoses the one bottleneck keeping a person's growth linear — then sells full Mindvalley Membership as the only fix that matches how bottlenecks actually work: chained across every area of life.

1Premise

Linear people build linear lives. Every strategy someone runs — in their business, career, craft, or health — runs through the person executing it. A capped operator caps every playbook. Growth becomes exponential only when the constraint itself is released, and the constraint is almost never out there. It's in one of four inner systems — and they're chained.

Low energy → foggy decisions → fear-based choices → weaker leverage → more on your plate → less recovery.
Fix one link alone and the chain pulls it back.

That chain is the whole argument, primed on every interstitial: because bottlenecks are interdependent, the answer is never one course — it's every area, in the right order. That's why the product is Membership, and why the pitch feels like a conclusion instead of an ad.

5The offer

  • Product: full Mindvalley Membership (110+ programs) — both tiers, one product story.
  • Pricing: Monthly $49 · Yearly $299 → $199 with $100 off (discount code lives server-side in the checkout link — the timer promises exactly what checkout shows). Per-day anchors: $1.63 vs $0.55.
  • Urgency: 15-minute persistent countdown (localStorage, same pattern as the live manifesting funnel).
  • Guarantee: 15-day money-back, framed as the week-one test on their Phase 1 program.
  • Exit ramp: non-buyers → membership explainer page, tagged, so the click is never wasted.

6Social proof standard

Every testimonial is a real student from stories.mindvalley.com — real name, profession, location, verbatim result, real photo (self-hosted). No fabrication, ever. Casting matches the branch: founders see Nora (+300% sales), professionals see Sarah (3 promotions), self-growth sees Camille (3 businesses + 45 lbs + marriage — the interdependency proof). ~20 stories per branch already mined and verified for Business + Self.

7Data & attribution

  • Checkout clicks stamped utm_source=quiz, utm_medium=exponential-you, utm_content=tier, utm_term=bottleneck; inbound gclid/fbclid forwarded end-to-end.

  • Per-screen analytics event required at launch — we optimize drop-off like MadMuscles or we're guessing.
